# 8. Magical Thinking

**Problem**: Hoping model will figure it out without explicit rules.

**Anti-pattern**:
- No instruction priorities
- No conflict descriptions
- "Model will guess"

**Countermeasures**:
- Explicit "laws of robotics" - clear hierarchy
- Conflict resolution rules - what to do on contradictions
- Operational rules - specific instructions
- Examples - show how to apply rules
