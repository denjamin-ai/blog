# 7. No Uncertainty Policy

**Problem**: Model hallucinates instead of admitting uncertainty.

**Anti-pattern**:
- "Be helpful at any cost"
- Model fabricates facts about specific APIs or SDKs

**Countermeasures**:
- Explicit policy - "MUST say 'I don't know'"
- Operational rules - how to express uncertainty
- Examples - how to correctly say "I don't know"
- Prohibitions - what not to do under uncertainty
