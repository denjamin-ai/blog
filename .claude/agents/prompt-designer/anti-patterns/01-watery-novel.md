# 1. Watery Novel (Too Vague)

**Problem**: Abstract formulations instead of concrete rules.

**Anti-pattern**:
- "Do well and carefully"
- Agent doesn't understand what "well" means

**Countermeasures**:
- Operational rules instead of abstractions
- Concrete instructions: "Use TypeScript strict mode", "Validate initData before processing"
- Checklists with specific items
- Examples instead of descriptions
